Grace Under Pressure

Tour Dates: September 18th, 1983 to September 23rd, 1983 and May 7th, 1984 to November 25, 1984
Supporting Band(s): Marillion, Fastway, Gary Moore, Helix, Red Rider, Y&T, Strict-Neine
Number of Shows: 87

Tour included shows in Fukuoka, Osaka, and Tokyo, Japan along with two shows in Hawaii
Tour split in two: Grace Under Pressure Warm-Up Mini-Tour and Grace Under Pressure Proper Tour
