Tour of the Hemispheres

Tour Dates: October 14th, 1978 to June 4th, 1979
Supporting Band(s): Max Webster, Ambrosia, April Wine, Golden Earring, The Good Rats, Head East, Molly Hatchet, Pat Travers, Starz, Saxon, Wild Horses, UFO, Streetheart
Number of Shows: 143

Final show in Geleen, Holland was for the Pink Pop Festival where The Police, Peter Tosh, Elvis Costello, Dire Straits, Average White Band, and Massada also performed
